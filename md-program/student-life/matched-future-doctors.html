Exclusive Housing. Inclusive environment. 

Welcome to The Villages at Coverley, the vibrant living and learning Ross University School of Medicine housing community for students.  

Located a short ride from the RUSM campus, The Villages is a one-stop destination for studying, shopping and socializing. Enjoy the following: 

  • Daily shuttle to and from the RUSM campus 

  • 4-month leases with no deposit (most Barbados rentals require one-year minimum leases — plus deposit and first and last month’s rent) 

  • 24/7 onsite security 

  • On-site RUSM events and intramural sports 

  • Student group meeting space and student lounge 

  • RUSM Campus Offices including Counseling Services and Campus Life 

  • NEW $125/monthly credit from RUSM for electricity, water, and internet (average monthly utilities cost: $142) 

  • Construction built to withstand Category 5 hurricanes 

 

FRIENDLY FACES, EXCITING PLACES 

The Villages at Coverley were designed with a student-centric focus. When you need a break from studying, take a short walk to the Town Centre for a variety of local attractions:  

  • Fresh market grocery store 

  • Medical clinic and pharmacy 

  • Delicious, authentic restaurants and eateries 

  • Convenient banking services 

  • Fitness center featuring group classes 

Ross University School of Medicine Student Housing and Amenities

Make yourself at home.

YOUR ROOMMATES, YOUR CHOICE

Already have roommates in mind? No problem — simply put in a request with our student services team for your desired roommates and fill out a few required forms. Otherwise, we’re happy to assign you a same-gender roommate.* 

*Ross University School of Medicine housing only assigns same-gender roommates. If you and someone of another gender select each other as roommates, you may be placed in a 3- or 4-bedroom unit as space permits. This living arrangement may only be maintained if all agreed parties remain enrolled and on-campus. 

Ross Medical School Housing 3-BEDROOM OPTIONS

Tier 1: Bedroom and private bathroom (95-110 square feet) 

Tier 3: Bedroom and shared bathroom (95-110 square feet) 

Ross Medical School Housing 4-BEDROOM OPTIONS

Tier 2: Bedroom and private bathroom (110-125 square feet) 

Tier 4: Bedroom and shared bathroom (95-110 square feet) 

Ross Medical School Housing FAMILY-FRIENDLY RESIDENCES

The Villages offers 2 convenient options for Ross School of Medicine students who wish to live with a partner, child or other dependent: 

Ross University On-Campus Family housing: 

  • Specially discounted 3BR family units (limited quantity, subject to availability) 
  • Maximum occupancy: 2 adults, 5 people total 
  • If confirmed, you will be guaranteed a family unit for the length of your enrollment (continued documentation of companion/dependent required) 

General student housing: 

  • Tier 1 or Tier 2 bedroom (with private bath) in a traditional student unit 
  • Consent of all roommates is required beforehand 
Ross Medical School Housing BUDGET-FRIENDLY RATES

Single student pricing (per semester; prices in USD)* 

  • Tier 1: Bedroom and private bath in 3BR unit = $4,840 
  • Tier 2: Bedroom and private bath in 4BR unit = $4,640 
  • Tier 3: Bedroom and shared bath in 3BR unit = $4,240 
  • Tier 4: Bedroom and shared bath in 4BR unit = $4,040 

Family pricing (per semester, in USD)* 

  • 3BR unit with private bath = $7,500 
  • NEW: $840 utilities credit per semester! 

Rent includes property management, exterior and interior maintenance, personal property insurance, deep cleaning between each semester, Wi-Fi, trash pickup, monthly utility allotment (water, electric, and sewer), parking and gym membership.  

*Non-student companions approved to share a bedroom in a general student unit pay a 

$500 per semester amenities and administration fee. 

PET-FRIENDLY RULES
  • Pets allowed with special pet housing applications  
  • Dogs or cats under 40 pounds permitted with approval 
  • One pet per student (maximum 2 per unit) 
  • Fish are welcome (5-gallon tank maximum) 

 

 

LIVE OFF-CAMPUS  

Most Ross University School of Medicine housing students live at the Villages at Coverley, but we understand if you would rather live elsewhere. After the first semester, students have the option of finding alternative housing off-campus. 

Please keep in mind that access to these perks are only available to The Villages residents: 

  • Complimentary daily shuttle to and from campus 

  • Affordable rent — plus no deposit required 

  • Complimentary $125/month credit for electricity, water, and internet 

  • Fully furnished housing (including dishes, appliances and more) 

  • 24/7 onsite security 

  • Student group meeting space and student lounge 

  • On-site campus offices, including Counseling Services and Campus Life 

  • On-site RUSM events and intramural sports 

  • Construction built to withstand Category 5 hurricanes
